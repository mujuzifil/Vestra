# Stage 18.1 — Navigation Redesign

## Existing Navigation

| Position | Label | Destination | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------------|-------|
| 1 | Home | `/` | — |
| 2 | About Us | `/about` | — |
| 3 | Products | `/products` | Dropdown of 4 products |
| 4 | Distributor | `/distributor` | Interrupts shopping journey |
| 5 | Contact Us | `/contact` | — |
| Icon | Cart | cart drawer | Visible but no product-level CTA |
| Icon | Account | `/account` or `/admin` | Hidden behind icon |

### Problems identified
- `Distributor` was a top-level nav item with equal weight to `Products`, signalling that applying to resell was a normal step in browsing.
- No entry point for corporate/wholesale buyers.
- `Account` was only an icon, making account discovery harder on mobile.

## Proposed Navigation

| Position | Label | Destination | Rationale |
|----------|-------|-------------|-----------|
| 1 | Home | `/` | — |
| 2 | About Us | `/about` | — |
| 3 | Products | `/products` | Retail discovery remains prominent |
| 4 | Bulk Orders | `/bulk-orders` | Dedicated wholesale quotation path |
| 5 | Become a Distributor | `/distributor` | Label now describes an optional business application |
| 6 | Contact Us | `/contact` | — |
| 7 | Account | `/account` | Text link makes account discovery explicit |
| Icon | Cart | cart drawer | Unchanged; cart is always accessible |

## Mobile Menu
The same seven text links are rendered in the mobile full-screen menu so phone users have identical journey separation.

## Implementation
Updated `frontend/components/navigation/navbar.tsx`:
- Renamed `Distributor` → `Become a Distributor`.
- Added `{ label: "Bulk Orders", href: "/bulk-orders" }`.
- Added `{ label: "Account", href: "/account" }` to `navLinks`.

## Result
Retail, distributor, and bulk workflows are visually separated and equally accessible without one interrupting the other.
