# Stage 18.2 — Shopping Cart Report

## Add to Cart
- Implemented on product detail pages, product listing cards, and home featured products.
- Quantity selector on product detail pages defaults to 1 and respects minimum quantity.
- Clicking Add to Cart triggers a toast notification: "Added to cart — {quantity} × {product}".
- Cart badge animates when item count increases.
- Out-of-stock items cannot be added.

## Buy Now
- On product detail pages, Buy Now adds the selected quantity to cart and navigates to `/checkout`.
- Preserves selected quantity.
- Shows toast on success; errors are toasted on failure.

## Full Cart Page (`/cart`)
- Displays product image, name, SKU, unit price, quantity, stock status, and line total.
- Quantity controls: +/- buttons and direct number input.
- Remove item with browser confirmation.
- Save for later placeholder (disabled with tooltip).
- Order summary with subtotal, shipping placeholder, tax placeholder, estimated total.
- Continue Shopping and Proceed to Checkout buttons.
- Clear Cart with confirmation.

## Mini Cart (Drawer)
- Shows product image, name, SKU, price, stock status.
- Quantity +/- and direct input.
- Remove item button.
- Subtotal and Checkout button.
- New "View Cart" link.
- Improved empty state with `EmptyCart` component.
- Closes on overlay click, Escape key, and locks body scroll when open.

## Cart Persistence
- Guest cart is stored in `localStorage` under `vestra_cart`.
- Survives refresh, navigation, and browser restart.
- Logged-in users have their cart persisted server-side.
- On login, guest cart items are merged to the server cart once, then local storage is cleared.

## Quantity Management
- Minimum quantity: 1.
- Maximum quantity: available stock.
- Direct input validates numeric values.
- Removing at quantity 1 requires explicit remove action or confirmation on the full cart page.

## Feedback
- Toast notifications for: added, quantity updated, removed, cart cleared, errors, stock limit.
- Cart badge scale animation on count increase.
