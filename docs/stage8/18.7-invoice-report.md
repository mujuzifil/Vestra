# Stage 18.7 — Invoice Report

## Objective

Give distributors a dedicated invoice portal where they can view billed orders, see amount paid and balance due, and download PDF invoices.

## Invoice Portal

Distributor invoices are surfaced through `App\Http\Controllers\Api\V1\Distributor\InvoiceController` at:

- `GET /api/v1/distributor/invoices` — list all orders with an `invoice_number`
- `GET /api/v1/distributor/invoices/{invoice}` — view invoice details
- `GET /api/v1/distributor/invoices/{invoice}?download=1` — download the PDF

The controller scopes invoices to the authenticated distributor by checking `distributor_id`. Any attempt to access another distributor's invoice returns `403 Unauthorized`. This scoping is applied consistently across the portal, ensuring distributors only see their own financial records.

## Amount Paid / Balance Due

The `InvoiceResource` exposes:

- `amount` — the order total
- `amount_paid` — computed by the `Order::amountPaid()` method
- `balance_due` — computed by the `Order::outstandingBalance()` method
- `payment_status` and `payment_method`
- `subtotal`, `tax_amount`, `total_amount`
- Order items, timestamps

This allows distributors to see exactly how much remains unpaid for each invoice without manual calculation. The outstanding balance is also reflected in the dashboard's outstanding balance card, giving distributors a single view of total exposure across all invoices.

## PDF Download

PDF generation reuses the existing `InvoicePdfService`. When the `download` query parameter is present, the controller:

1. Calls `$this->service->save($invoice)` to generate the PDF.
2. Streams the file contents as a download with the filename `{invoice_number}.pdf` and `Content-Type: application/pdf`.

This keeps invoice rendering consistent with the retail customer invoice experience while remaining scoped to distributor orders. Distributor orders are assigned a `VST-D-YYYYMMDD-####` invoice number during checkout, making them easily distinguishable from retail orders.

## Frontend

The invoice list page at `/distributor/invoices` (`frontend/app/distributor/invoices/invoices-page-client.tsx`) displays a searchable grid of invoice cards. Each card shows:

- Invoice number and date
- Order status
- Amount, amount paid, and balance due
- Links to view detail and download PDF

The detail page at `/distributor/invoices/[id]` (`frontend/app/distributor/invoices/[id]/invoice-detail-client.tsx`) presents the full invoice breakdown, including line items, tax, total, payment status, and direct download action. Both list and detail pages use the same data source, so amounts remain consistent across views. Because invoices are derived from distributor-channel orders, the portal does not require a separate invoice entity—orders serve as the invoice record with the generated `invoice_number`.
