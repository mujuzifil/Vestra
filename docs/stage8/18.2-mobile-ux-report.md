# Stage 18.2 — Mobile UX Report

## Cart Drawer
- Drawer uses full width on mobile (`w-full`) and `max-w-md` on larger screens.
- Touch targets for quantity buttons are at least 32×32 px.
- Remove button is easily tappable.
- Body scroll is locked while drawer is open to prevent background interaction.
- Escape key closes the drawer.

## Full Cart Page
- Responsive grid switches from 1 column on mobile to 3 columns on desktop.
- Cart item cards stack vertically on small screens.
- Product image, name, SKU, price, quantity, and total remain readable.
- Quantity controls are inline and tappable.
- Order summary sticks below the item list on mobile and becomes sticky on desktop.
- Continue Shopping and Clear Cart buttons stack on mobile.

## Checkout Entry
- Proceed to Checkout button is full-width on mobile.
- Drawer checkout button is prominent and tappable.

## Recommendations
- Consider increasing quantity button size to 44×44 px on very small screens.
- Add haptic feedback on supported devices when adding to cart.
- Test on actual devices to ensure drawer swipe-to-close feels natural.
