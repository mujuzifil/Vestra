# Stage 18.7 — Analytics Report

## Objective

Provide distributors with self-service analytics about their purchasing activity, quotations, and credit utilization, with selectable reporting periods.

## Analytics Endpoint

`App\Http\Controllers\Api\V1\Distributor\AnalyticsController` serves `GET /api/v1/distributor/analytics`. The endpoint accepts an optional `period` query parameter:

- `7_days`
- `30_days` (default)
- `90_days`
- `this_year`

The controller converts the period into a start date and computes metrics scoped to the authenticated distributor.

## Metrics Returned

The `AnalyticsResource` returns:

- **Summary counts:** total orders, total revenue, total quotes, pending quotes, average order value, orders count in period, accepted quotations count.
- **Growth:** month-over-month growth percentage compared to the preceding equal-length period.
- **Orders by status:** counts for every `OrderStatus` case within the selected period.
- **Revenue by month:** rolling 6-month revenue totals.
- **Top products:** up to 5 best-selling products by quantity, with total revenue.
- **Financial health:** pending payments total and credit utilization percentage.

All monetary values are formatted to two decimal places for display.

## Data Sources & Calculation

The analytics controller queries only orders and quotations that belong to the authenticated distributor. Top products are computed by flattening order items from the selected period, grouping by `product_id`, summing quantities and line totals, and taking the top five by quantity. Revenue by month aggregates order totals by year and month across the last six months. Orders by status uses a `GROUP BY status` query and maps every `OrderStatus` case to a count, ensuring the response is complete even for statuses with zero orders.

## Frontend

The analytics page at `/distributor/analytics` (`frontend/app/distributor/analytics/analytics-page-client.tsx`) renders:

- A top row of stat cards for total orders, total revenue, total quotes, and average order value.
- Simple horizontal bar charts for revenue by month and orders by status.
- A top products grid showing product name, quantity sold, and revenue.

The layout is responsive: stat cards use a 2-column grid on mobile and 4 columns on large screens; charts stack on small viewports.

## Period Filters

The backend period matching and frontend display make it easy to extend the UI with a period selector. Currently the default period is 30 days and the endpoint supports four presets without requiring additional frontend state.

The `useDistributorAnalytics` hook caches results under the key `distributor-analytics` and refetches on page revisit, keeping the dashboard responsive while still reflecting recent orders.
